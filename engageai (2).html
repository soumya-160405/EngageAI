Key Dataset Insights
Reels Dominate
Reels achieve 4.8% avg engagement — 14% higher than static images. Video content is consistently the top performer.
CTA Power
Posts with a call-to-action outperform those without by 23% on average across all media types and platforms.
Hashtag Sweet Spot
6–10 hashtags delivers the strongest reach. Beyond 15, engagement drops by up to 20% — less is more.
Caption Length
100–150 character captions correlate with peak engagement. Very short and very long captions both underperform.
Weekend Wins
Saturday and Sunday posts achieve the highest engagement, followed by Friday. Monday is the weakest day.
Creator Advantage
Creator accounts achieve ~15% higher engagement than brand accounts with comparable follower counts.
